Places You Must Visit

prithvi cafe

i visited in december 2018

Quaint little restaurant tucked inside a by-lane of Juhu. Blink and you will miss the entrace. So be alert enough to spot the black sign that says "Prithvi cafe" while you are on the main road. The retaurant has a reasonably good menu at reasonable prices. The food is delicious, the servins are quite large and some dishes can be shared by 2 people. Attracts quete a trendy crowd of aspiring celevrites on show days. Be sure to book in advance to avoid disappointments. On other days, the crowd there is pretty hep & happening. Hosts the famous "Prithvi theater fest", which you must attend if possible.



Review on Movies

Avengers Endgame
Excellent film to culmiate MCU ride

I watched it today with my kids and they loved it. You should have a minimal previous understanding of the story, or you will mis 40% of the fun. You should watch Infinity War, Civil War, Black Panther, Age of Ultron, Ant-Man and the Wasp before watch Avengers: Endgame. The entire movie pays homage to most of the other Marvel superhero movies. You will enjoy it a lot. There is, at the beginning of the movie, a (spoiler alert) head dismerberment that is quite impressive. The movie is based on the most-talked action-packed Sci-fi series. The movie shows how the Avengers assemble once more in order to reverse Thanos' Actions and restore balance to the universe. The movie is lengthy, and it does run for 3 hours all worthwhile.
